Open Sea

Open Sea - The game

Download Download View Open Sea on GitHub

Open Sea is a sandbox RPG written in Rust using the Piston library. Sail the open sea, explore the world, and play together with your friends.

Open Sea is still in its early stages.

Contribute

Open an issue, or fork the repo and send a pull request. Contributions are much appreciated.
